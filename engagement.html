How We Work

Your Vision, Our Mission

VoltForce offers a range of partnership options built for different stages of growth — from single high-impact hires to full-scale team buildouts.

Each model is transparent, performance-based, and designed to deliver quality without compromise.

Here's what you can expect:

01: Strategy & Search Launch
We start with a deep-dive into your company, culture, and technical requirements. Then we map the talent landscape — who's available, what competitors are paying, and what makes your opportunity compelling — before launching a targeted search across our network.
02: Vetting & Shortlisting
Every candidate goes through thorough screening — technical fit, cultural alignment, motivation, and compensation expectations. You only see candidates we'd stake our reputation on. No resume dumps, no filler.
03: Interviews & Coordination
We handle all scheduling, candidate prep, and structured feedback collection after every round. Your team focuses on evaluating talent, not chasing calendars.
04: Offer, Close & Beyond
When you find the right person, we help you close them — market-informed offer structuring, negotiation guidance, and counteroffer navigation. And our job doesn't end at the signed offer. We follow up during onboarding to ensure a smooth transition and long-term fit.

How Pricing Works

A quarterly retainer covers your dedicated recruitment capacity and all operational support — sourcing, screening, coordination, and pipeline management across your open roles.

When we source and place a candidate from our network, a reduced placement fee applies — significantly below industry standard.

The part that changes the math: Candidates that come through your channels — job boards, referrals, inbound applications — we screen, shortlist, and manage at no additional placement fee. The retainer covers it.

The stronger your employer brand grows, the more value you extract from the partnership.
